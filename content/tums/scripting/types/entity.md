This is the Tums variation of the Minecraft Entity. Here are all of it's *unique* properties:
- `name` - The name of the entity
- `nametag` - The nametag name of the entity
- `uuid` - Entity's UUID
- `x` - The X coordinate of the entity
- `y` - The Y coordinate of the entity
- `z` - The Z coordinate of the entity
- `yaw` - The yaw of the entity
- `pitch` - The pitch of the entity
